Frequently Asked Questions

Is the calculator free to use?

Yes, all our calculators and educational content are completely free to use. We believe in making statistical tools accessible to everyone.

Do you store my calculation data?

No, all calculations are performed locally in your browser. We do not store or transmit any of the data you enter into our calculators.

Can I use this for academic research?

Absolutely! Our tools are designed to be academically rigorous. However, we recommend verifying critical results with additional methods.

What browsers are supported?

Our calculator works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. JavaScript must be enabled for full functionality.

How accurate are the calculations?

Our calculations use standard statistical formulas and JavaScript's built-in math functions. Results are accurate to the precision of standard computer arithmetic.

Can I integrate your calculator into my website?

We're exploring options for embedding and API access. Please contact us to discuss your specific integration needs.
